Privacy Policy for Line Animator

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Line Animator, developed by Zix, handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Line Animator does not collect personal information from users.

The app does not require users to provide personal details such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location

All animation projects created in the app are stored locally on the user's device.

2. Permissions Used

Storage Permission

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

These permissions allow the app to:

  • Save animation projects
  • Export images or videos
  • Load images from the user's device

The app does not access or collect personal files beyond what the user chooses to open or export.

Notification Permission

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS

This permission may be used to send notifications such as app updates or important information related to the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services provided by Google Play Services.

These services may collect anonymous information such as:

  • Device information
  • App performance data
  • Crash reports

This information is used to improve app stability and performance.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising

Currently, Line Animator does not display advertisements.

However, future versions of the app may include advertisements. These ads may collect anonymous device information to show relevant advertisements.

Learn more about Google advertising policies:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Storage

All user-created animation projects, images, and exported files are stored locally on the user's device. The developer does not collect, store, or transmit this data to external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Line Animator is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
